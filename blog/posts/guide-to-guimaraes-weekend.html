A relaxed weekend in Guimaraes

By Martins Bungalow Retreat March 17, 2026 5 min read

Guimaraes works especially well for guests who want culture without pressure. It is easy to explore at a calm pace, with enough history, cafes, and scenic spots to fill a weekend without overplanning every hour.

Day one: start with the historic centre

Begin with a slow walk through the old streets, taking time in the main squares and lanes where the city feels most atmospheric. This kind of half-day plan pairs well with a relaxed breakfast at the bungalow and a later lunch in town.

Day two: views and fresh air

Balance the heritage side of the city with a more open-air day. Scenic viewpoints, hillside walks, and short drives in the surrounding area help guests experience the wider landscape that makes this region such a strong base for a bungalow retreat.

Easy ideas to include in future guide posts

  • Best time of day to photograph the old centre
  • Family-friendly stops that fit around lunch or naps
  • Indoor alternatives for rainy afternoons
  • How far key attractions are from the bungalow by car

Stay tip: if this sounds like your kind of weekend, staying just outside the centre gives you the best of both worlds: easy access and more room to unwind.
